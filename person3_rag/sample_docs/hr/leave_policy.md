# Employee Leave Policy

## Annual Vacation
Full-time employees accrue 22 working days of paid vacation per calendar year.
Vacation accrues monthly and unused days may be carried over until March 31 of
the following year, after which they expire.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to up to 15 paid sick days per year. A medical
certificate is required for any sick leave longer than 3 consecutive days.

## Medical Leave Waivers
Extended medical leave beyond the standard allowance requires a medical_leave
waiver. Required documents: a signed physician's statement, the expected return
date, and the employee's manager approval. Requests are reviewed by HR before
escalation to a human approver.
